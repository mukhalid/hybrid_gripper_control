Individual Motor Control:
move_motor(motor_id, position) - Move single motor to specific position
open_motor(motor_id) - Open single motor
close_motor(motor_id) - Close single motor
get_motor_position(motor_id) - Read single motor position




Simultaneous Motor Control:
move_all_motors(position) - Move all motors to the same position
move_motors_individually(positions_dict) - Move each motor to different positions
open_all_motors() - Open all motors
close_all_motors() - Close all motors
get_all_positions() - Read all motor positions
stop_all_motors() - Stop all motors at current positions
